Originally hailing from southwest Missouri, I currently call San Francisco my home. I have a passion for code and the design of software platforms. Once upon a time my day job was as a senior application engineer for a small startup called Minted.com. There we pioneered the concept of high-end crowd-sourced print-on-demand stationery through the form of a socialized e-commerce platform. After that I moved on to become an ops engineer for the west coast branch of the FirstGiving.com group.

Much of my spare time is taken up by my growing photography and beer brewing obsessions as well as my various other engineering side projects.
